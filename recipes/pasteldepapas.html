Pastel de papas

How to make a Pastel de papas.

This is a typical argentinian recipe. We often eat this in family reunions (during winter).

First, we are going to need:

  • Grinded beef
  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Paprika
  • Milk
  • Butter

    Step by step:
  1. Boil the potatoes, and in the meanwhile cut the onions and the paprika into small pieces and cook it with the meat. We also need to boil the eggs.
  2. After everything is cooked, smash the potatoes, add some milk and butter and stir it.
  3. Then, grab a recipient, put one lay of puree, then the meat, and more puree on top. Pre-heat the oven at 220Â°C and introduce the Pastel de papas into the oven for 20 minutes.
  4. After this, take it out of the oven and serve it. Enjoy!
