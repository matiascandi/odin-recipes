Arroz con pollo

This is a recipe we could make every week! It is so tasty!

How to make an Arroz con pollo:

First, we are going to need:

  • Chicken
  • Rise
  • Onions
  • Azafran
  • Paprika

Step by step guide:

  1. First, we need to cut all the vegetables and cook the rise
  2. Then, we "sealed" the chicken
  3. After that, we put all together and we serve it with red wine
