Milanesas con papas fritas

This is a recipe we could make every single day! We love milanesas!

How to make milanesas con papas fritas:

First, we are going to need:

  • Beef/li>
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Pan rallado

Step by step guide:

  1. First, we need to cut the potatoes and fried them
  2. Also, we have to put the beef into the eggs and then to the pan rallado
  3. After that, we put the milanesas into the oven or we could also fry them
  4. That's it! We have milanesas con papas fritas
